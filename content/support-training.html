Add a resource

Anyone who has developed a Prometheus training program or offers related services can add themselves to this page if they meet the following requirements.

For courses and training programs:
  • The course must have a publicly available syllabus or outline on the page linked
  • If the course is instructor led, you must provide credentials (a link to your LinkedIn profile or resume will suffice)
For consultants or businesses:
  • Consultants must provide credentials (a link to your LinkedIn profile or resume will suffice)
  • Businesses must outline what Prometheus-related services they provide on the page linked

To submit, open a pull request against this page in the prometheus/docs repository. Include all the information requested in the PR description.
